### The 10-Step Insurance Claims Flow (Customer-Facing Copy):

**Step 1 — Document the Damage Immediately**
Take photos of every panel, every dent, from multiple angles. Date-stamp them. If possible, note the storm date and your city/ZIP. This is your evidence. Don't wash the car yet.

**Step 2 — Get a Free Professional Inspection First**
Before you call your insurer, get an independent inspection from Dent Boyz. We give you an honest assessment: how many dents, which panels are affected, and whether your car is a strong PDR candidate. This puts you in a stronger position with your adjuster.

**Step 3 — Contact Your Insurance Company**
Call your insurer's claims line or file online. Major Texas carriers:
| Insurer | Claims Line | Online |
|---|---|---|
| State Farm | 1-800-SF-CLAIM (1-800-732-5246) | statefarm.com |
| USAA | 1-800-531-8722 | usaa.com |
| Allstate | 1-800-255-7828 | allstate.com |
| Progressive | 1-800-776-4737 | progressive.com |
| Farmers | 1-800-435-7764 | farmers.com |
| GEICO | 1-800-207-7847 | geico.com |

You need: policy number, date of loss, location, description of damage. You do NOT need to commit to any repair shop yet.

**Step 4 — Understand Your Deductible**
Your comprehensive deductible is what you pay out of pocket before insurance covers the rest. Common deductibles in Texas: $250, $500, $1,000, $2,500. If your deductible equals or exceeds the repair cost, it may not be worth filing. We can help you do this math before you decide.

**What is Deductible Assistance?**
Some PDR shops (including Dent Boyz) offer to cover a portion of your deductible as part of doing business. This is legal in Texas as long as it's disclosed. Ask us about our current deductible assistance program.

**Step 5 — Schedule the Insurance Adjuster**
Your insurer will send an adjuster (in person or via photo AI tool like CCC One or Xactimate) to estimate the damage. This typically takes 3–7 business days after filing.

**Step 6 — Review the Estimate**
The adjuster produces a line-item estimate. Bring this to us. If we find additional damage during repair (very common with hail), we file a supplemental claim on your behalf. You don't have to fight that battle alone.

**Step 7 — Approve the Repair**
Once you're satisfied with the estimate, authorize us to begin. We coordinate directly with your insurer for payment. You pay only your deductible (or less, with deductible assistance).

**Step 8 — Repair Begins**
Most hail PDR repairs take 1–5 business days depending on severity. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront. We can arrange a loaner or rental coordination.

**Step 9 — Final Inspection + Delivery**
Before we return your vehicle, we do a full quality check under specialized lighting. Every dent gone, every panel factory-smooth.

**Step 10 — Payment + Paperwork**
Insurance pays us directly (minus your deductible). You receive a copy of the completed claim, our warranty documentation, and your vehicle back looking like the storm never happened.

### What PDR Shops Can and Cannot Do with Insurance:
**CAN:** File supplemental claims for damage found during repair, work directly with insurers for payment, offer deductible assistance (disclosed)
**CANNOT:** Inflate estimates, waive deductibles without disclosure, guarantee specific payouts from insurers

### Texas Total Loss Threshold:
In Texas, a vehicle is considered a total loss when repair cost exceeds **100% of the vehicle's Actual Cash Value (ACV)**. Hail damage rarely totals newer vehicles unless damage is catastrophic. If your insurer declares a total loss, you can dispute the ACV with comparable sales data — we can help you understand your options.
